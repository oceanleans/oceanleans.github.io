Cookies and Analytics

OceanLeans does not currently use advertising trackers or on-site analytics tools through this website's own code. If analytics, cookies, or similar tools are added later, this policy will be updated.

Sharing of Information

OceanLeans does not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers who help operate the website or when disclosure is required by law.

Data Retention

Information is kept only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose it was provided, unless a longer retention period is required by law or needed to protect legitimate interests.

Your Choices

If you would like to ask about, correct, or request deletion of personal information you have provided, you can reach out through the Contact page.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
